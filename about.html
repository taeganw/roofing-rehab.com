Our Story

Built on Trust, Not Sales Tactics

Roofing Rehab is a family-owned residential roofing business serving North Idaho. We're the go-to experts for roof repairs and rehabilitation in our community, and we built this company on a simple belief: homeowners deserve affordable roofing solutions that never compromise on quality.

Our mission is to build lasting client relationships rooted in trust and transparency — no pressure, no upsells, just honest work. Every project, whether it's a small repair or a full re-roof, gets the same level of craftsmanship and attention to detail.

We're not a national franchise chasing volume. We're your neighbors, and we treat every roof like it's protecting our own family.
