Our Roofing & Exterior Services

Quality workmanship on every project, from a single repair to a full new-construction build.

Residential Re-Roofs

Complete roof tear-offs and expert shingle installation using trusted IKO products. We handle every step — from stripping old material and inspecting the deck to installing underlayment and new shingles that hold up through North Idaho winters.

Roofing and exterior systems for new builds, from single-family homes to larger commercial projects. We work directly with builders and homeowners to get the roof system right from the ground up.

Adding a room, garage, or second story? We seamlessly tie new roofing into your existing structure so the finished product looks — and performs — like it was always one roof.

Additions Remodels Seamless Tie-Ins
